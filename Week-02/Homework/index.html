Avocado Toast w/ Eggs

Avocado toast is a healthy breakfast.
It contains a decent amount of fiber, potassium, vitamin E, and iron.
It is easy to make and you can also add more ingredients, like a tomato slice or fried egg.
And we will be adding a fried egg to our avocado toast.

Ingredients:

  • Bread (2 slices)
  • Avocado (1 whole)
  • Eggs (2)
  • Butter or Cooking Spray
  • Optional:
    • Salt
    • Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat pan and then place butter (or spray your cooking spray). Make sure that the melted butter (or spray) coats the entire surface. This helps prevent the eggs from sticking.
  2. Once the pan is coated with melted butter (or spray), crack the eggs into the pan separately, keeping the yolks intact. Let the eggs cook.
  3. Toast the bread slices, but remove them so that they are slightly toasted with a bit of crunch. (Or toast them as much as you want).
  4. Cut the avocado in half and spread half of the avocado on each slice of bread.
  5. By now, the eggs should be ready to flip. If they don't move easily, let them cook a bit longer. When ready, use a spatula to separate the egg whites if they've joined forces, then flip them.
  6. Let the eggs cook for just under a minute. (Or cook for longer if you want a firmer yolk). Once done, place them on top of the avocado-coated bread slices.
  7. Add salt, pepper. Or don't as this is optional.
  8. You are done.
