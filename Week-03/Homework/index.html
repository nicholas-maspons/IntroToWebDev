Ingredients

  • Sub roll
  • Cream cheese
  • Pickles
  • Dill
  • Eggs
  • Bird (from the window)
  • Cucumber
  • Roma Tomato
  • Sweet yellow onion (organic)
  • Tears (for salt)
  • Meat (prepared sous vide)
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Baconnnnn
  • Lobster soul

Instructions

  1. In a pot large enough to fit a lobster, bathe your onion and tomato.
  2. Remove the onion and tomato, then heat the water.
  3. Place the meat in a sealed bag with rosemary and thyme, then submerge in hot water to cook sous vide at 135F.
  4. Slice the bread in half horizontally.
  5. Toast the bread with a kitchen torch.
  6. Spread cream cheese on both pieces of bread.
  7. Place pickles on the bottom slice and dill on the top.
  8. Place diced boiled eggs and bird from the window (cooked chicken breast) on the bottom slice.
  9. Add cucumber, tomato, and onion slices to the bottom slice.
  10. Splash some tears for salt on both pieces of bread.
  11. Place the meat, followed by 3 slices of bacon, on the bottom slice.
  12. Insert the lobster into the pot of hot water. Once its soul rises, fan it toward and into your sandwich.
  13. Unite both sides of your sandwich, which will emit a glow if properly prepared.
